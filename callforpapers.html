Call for Papers

ICBSII 2023 is an opportunity for medical engineering professionals from around the world who are involved in the field of healthcare to submit their original research papers which have not been submitted elsewhere in other conferences / journals. All registered and presented papers in the conference will be published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library . Best papers will be awarded under each session.

  • Biosignals

    • 1. Biomedical Signal Processing.
    • 2. Cardiovascular & Respiratory Systems Engineering.
    • 3. Cognitive Neuroscience.
    • 4. Brain-Computer Interface.
    • 5. Speech Signal Processing.
    • 6. Machine learning algorithms for Signal analysis
    • 7. All-pervasive Wireless Systems for Health Applications.

  • Medical Instrumentation

    • 1. Mobile Health and Wearable Sensor Networks.
    • 2. Advances in Biomedical Instrumentation.
    • 3. Body Area Network.
    • 4. Non-contact Physiological Measurement System.
    • 5. Wearable, Outdoor and Home-based Applications.
    • 6. Printable Electronics for Healthcare Applications
    • 7. Tissue Engineering / Biomaterials.
    • 8. Drug Delivery Systems / Nano - Bioengineering
    • 9. 3D Bioprinting.

  • Medical Images

    • 1. Biomedical Imaging and Data Visualization.
    • 2. Computer-Aided and Automated Diagnosis.
    • 3. Medical Image Mining & Retrieval system.
    • 4. Optimization techniques.
    • 5. Augmented/Virtual Reality.
    • 6. Biomedical Optics and Biophotonics

  • Medical Data analytics

    • 1. Development of tools for high dimensional medical data.
    • 2. Data Collection and Data Mining in e-Health Applications.
    • 3. E-Health Software Design.
    • 4. In-memory Computation in Healthcare.
    • 5. Big Data Analytics for Health Data Management
    • 6. Point of Care Diagnosis.
    • 7. Healthcare Telemetry and Telemedicine.
    • 8. Remote Diagnosis and Patient Management.
    • 9. Internet of Things (IoT) and Fog Computing in Healthcare 5 G for Healthcare Application.

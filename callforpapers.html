Call for Papers

ICBSII 2024 is an opportunity for engineering professionals from around the world who are involved in the field of healthcare to submit their original research papers which have not been submitted elsewhere in other conferences / journals. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements. Best papers will be awarded under each session.

  • Track 1 - Biomedical Signal Processing

    • 1. Speech and Audio Signal Processing.
    • 2. Machine learning algorithms for Signal Analysis.
    • 3. Neural Signal Processing.
    • 4. Computational modeling of Biosignals and Systems.
    • 5. Cardiovascular & Respiratory signals.
    • 6. Brain Computer Interface

  • Track 2 - AI & DS for Healthcare

    • 1. Biomedical Imaging Technologies.
    • 2. Image Visualization Techniques.
    • 3. Medical Image Mining & Retrieval System.
    • 4. Cognitive Neuro Imaging and Processing.
    • 5. Image based Modeling.
    • 6. Video Analytics in Healthcare.

  • Track 3 - Data Science and Computing Technologies

    • 1. Computer-Aided and Automated Diagnosis.
    • 2. Bioinformatics.
    • 3. Physiological Modeling.
    • 4. XR in Healthcare.
    • 5. Big Data Analytics and Data Mining in e-Health.
    • 6. Telemetry and Telemedicine.

  • Track 4 - Biomedical Sensors, Instrumentation and Wearable Systems

    • 1. Medical Device Development.
    • 2. Biomechanics and Robotics.
    • 3. IoMT / Mobile Health and Wearable Sensor.
    • 4. Body Area Network and Point of Care Diagnosis.
    • 5. Non-contact Physiological Measurement System.
    • 6. Medical Optics and Biophotonics.

  • Track 5 - Materials for Bioscience

    • 1. Micro / Nano materials.
    • 2. Tissue Engineering / Biomaterials.
    • 3. Drug Delivery Systems.
    • 4. 3D printing / 3D Bioprinting.
    • 5. Additive Manufacturing for Healthcare.
    • 6. Gene therapy.

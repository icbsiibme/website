Submit Paper

The manuscript must satisfy the following requirements:

Authors can submit their manuscripts through the following link,

https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icbsii2020
 

  • An author of an accepted paper is required to register for the conference and present the paper at the conference. Non-refundable registration fees must be paid prior to the due date of registration. For authors with multiple accepted papers, one registration for each paper is required.
  • Prospective authors are invited to submit papers of five (5) to six (6) A4 pages (including tables, figures and references) in standard double-column format
  • A blind peer-review process will be used to evaluate all submitted papers. Each full registration for the conference will cover a maximum of one paper; each student registration will cover a single paper only. Extra paper, 2nd paper and onwards, must be registered separately.

  • The manuscript must be in English.
  • Hyperlinks should be removed from the URLs mentioned in the references.

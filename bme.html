About BME

The department of Biomedical Engineering was started in the year 2005 offering B.E Biomedical Engineering and M.E. Medical Electronics, since 2014. The U.G. the program is accredited by NBA and has been recognized as a research centre by Anna University in 2011 for pursuing M.S (By Research) and Ph.D. programmes. The department has well-equipped laboratories like Biomedical Instrumentation lab, Microbiology and Biochemistry lab, Diagnostic and Therapeutic lab, and Medical Software lab to enable the students and the scholars to pursue research in-house.

Several consultancy projects in biomechanics, biomaterials and artificial organs have been carried out for National and overseas clients.
